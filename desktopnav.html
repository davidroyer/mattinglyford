Mattingly Ford Title Service (MFTS) is a full-service regional title company with a staff of 30 very experienced real estate professionals, including four real estate attorneys. Our mission at MFTS is to provide our clients with the highest quality, most efficient, accurate and reliable closing services, in a timely manner, for a fair price.

We do our utmost to maintain the highest possible professional standards. We strive to maintain and bolster our good reputation in the community. This reputation is based upon ethical business practices and good service and meeting or exceeding our clients’ objectives and needs. We enjoy our work, and work hard to be an excellent service partner to our clients and contribute to their ultimate success.

MFTS has the technology and is organized, staffed and automated to handle volume and difficult closings within tight deadlines. We set deadlines and do whatever it takes to close a transaction. We have a “don’t-say-no” attitude that permeates our entire staff. We are cost-conscious, as well as results-oriented.

We work to accommodate our clients’ closing needs, big or small, from the occasional closing, to considerable volume. We are dedicated to keeping the closing process as simple as possible and have good communication with our clients. Our goal is to provide the outstanding personalized service that you and your customers expect.
