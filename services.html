Services We Offer

Mattingly Ford Title Services, LLC is a regional, professional real estate title examination and closing service. We take pride in our speed, accuracy, and flexibility. We have over 30 professional, experienced employees, who will add value to your lending products. We also have a staff of four real estate attorneys who assist the title and closing process and who are at your service to ensure that the title work and closings are done correctly. We are flexible and dove-tail our service schedule to meet your client’s needs. We pride ourselves in offering a competitive advantage backed by reliable, friendly service. You can count on MFTS to deliver on its promise of a professional and accurate title and closing every time. We guarantee your complete satisfaction on every closing... period.
